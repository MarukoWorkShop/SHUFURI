SHUFURI Privacy Policy SHUFURI 隐私政策

Last Updated: June 21, 2026最近更新：2026年6月21日

Welcome to SHUFURI (the "App"), a local-first typography and linguistic tech-stationery application designed for independent language learners and typography enthusiasts. 欢迎使用 SHUFURI（下称“本应用”），这是一款专为独立语言学习者和排版爱好者设计的本地优先排版与语言科技手账应用。

Our philosophy is rooted in "Use-and-Go" utility and absolute privacy. Therefore, our data privacy policy is simple: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. 我们的理念根植于“用后即走”的实用主义与绝对的隐私保护。因此，我们的数据隐私政策非常简单：我们不收集、不存储、亦不分享您的任何个人数据。

1. Zero Data Collection 1. 零数据收集

  • Linguistic Streams & Layouts: All text parsing, phonetic ruby annotations (such as {base:reading} micro-syntax), and typographic page calculations happen 100% locally on your device. We do not have servers, and we never upload your texts, lyrics, or generated study cards. 语言流与排版： 所有的文本解析、注音轨道（如 `{基字:读音}` 微语法）以及排版页面计算，100% 在您的本地设备上运行。我们没有服务器，绝不上传您的文本、歌词或生成的研习卡片。
  • Clipboard Data: The App may access your clipboard only when you explicitly trigger the paste function on the input screen to decode your stream. This data is processed transiently in the device memory and is never saved or transmitted. 剪贴板数据： 本应用仅在您明确触发输入界面的粘贴功能以解析密文流时，才会访问您的剪贴板。该数据仅在设备内存中作瞬时处理，绝不进行保存或传输。
  • Device Information: We do not track your device ID, IP address, or location. 设备信息： 我们不追踪您的设备 ID、IP 地址或地理位置。

2. Third-Party Services 2. 第三方服务

No Analytics: The App does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising platforms. 无数据分析： 本应用未集成任何第三方分析 SDK、追踪像素或广告平台。

External AI Clipboard (User-Driven): When you manually copy external encrypted data streams into the App, that transaction is handled entirely by you. The App remains a pure offline rendering container. 外部 AI 剪贴板（用户驱动）： 当您手动将外部加密数据流复制到本应用中时，该交互完全由您处理。本应用始终是一个纯粹的离线渲染容器。

3. Children's Privacy 3. 儿童隐私

Since we collect absolutely zero personal data, the App is inherently compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR. 鉴于我们收集的个人数据完全为零，本应用天生符合《儿童在线隐私保护法》（COPPA）及通用数据保护条例（GDPR）的要求。

4. Contact Us 4. 联系我们

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer directly within the open-source repository or developer channel. 如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请直接在开源仓库或开发者频道中联系开发者。
